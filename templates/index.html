Here at GROW we have three main principles:

  1. Eating well should never = boring food
  2. Seasonal produce is always best
  3. Plants make everything better

This is why we endeavour to change our menu every week, with a variety of dishes inspired by our travels around the world.
All produce used within the restaurant is sourced locally and we only eat what is currently in season in the UK.

Everything on the menu is vegetarian but can me made vegan and we design our dishes with nourishment in mind.

Founded in 2017 by two friends who absolutely adore food.
